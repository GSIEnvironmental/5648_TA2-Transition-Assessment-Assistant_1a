The TA2 Tool was funded as part of a SERDP Statement of Need titled “Quantitative Groundwater Plume Characterization to Support Transition Assessments”.   Several other projects were funded under this Statement of Need, and these projects also aim to improve our ability to identify transition points from active to more passive remedial measures, and to allow us to better assess the impacts of interim remedial measures.   

 

Links to the SERDP project pages are provided below for several of these projects.  The information found at these pages—which will continue to be updated as these projects progress—include tools, guidance, reports, and other publications that highlight key findings and benefits to DoD and other interested parties.
